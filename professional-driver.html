About the job
Company Description

Welcome to Wenzo, where luxury meets convenience! We are a premium ride-hailing and car rental app, offering a world of opulence and comfort at your fingertips. Say goodbye to ordinary rides and join us in elevating travel experiences.

Role Description

This is a full-time on-site role for a Professional Driver located in Accra, Ghana. The role encompasses various day-to-day tasks, including driving customers to their destinations safely and comfortably, maintaining and cleaning the vehicle, providing excellent customer service, and communicating with dispatch.

Qualifications

Class A Drivers license
Experience driving high-end/ luxury vehicles
Provide a reference from a past employer
Strong communication and customer service skills
An iPhone with data.
Google Maps Navigation Knowledge
Knowledge of traffic laws and regulations, familiarity with the Accra road network
Clean background check and driving record
Rideshare drivers experience (preferred)
At least 21 years of age.
No major traffic violations in the past 3 years.
Must be fluent in English.
Must be able to sit for long periods while operating a vehicle.
Must be able to lift and carry or otherwise move 50 pounds
Non-smoking.
Ability to read and understand GPS mapping.
Familiarity with electronic devices such as smartphones and tablets.
Able to make sound and quick decisions.
Punctual, demonstrating dependable attendance patterns.
Arrive well rested and alert when reporting for duty.
Maintain a clean, polished, and professional appearance.
Must live in or near East Legon.
Must be comfortable working 12 hour shifts.
